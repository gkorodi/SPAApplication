Favourite Place

The place is Nauthólsvík Geothermal Beach in Reykjavík, Iceland.
The small community beach is open year round, and is built on a geothermal spring, which keeps the outdoor bathing pool water warm without any external energy use.
Locals visit this hidden area for sailing, swimming and in general just to walk and bike around. Just on the outskirts of the capital it is never too busy, and the best part is? It is free.
